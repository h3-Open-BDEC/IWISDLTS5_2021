Goal of the Workshop

The goal of the workshop is to bring together researchers, industrialists, and students working in the state-of-the-art fields of the high performance computing of numerical simulation.
This workshop is closely related to Grant-in-Aid for Scientific Research(S) “h3-Open-BDEC: An Innovative Method for Integration of Simulation /Data/Learning in the Exascale/Post-Moore Era”

Topics of Interest

Peta-scale and Exa-scale Computing
Parallel and Distributed Computing
Numerical Libraries and Middleware
Performance Analysis
Fault Tolerance
Auto-tuning
